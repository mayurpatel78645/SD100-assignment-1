Corn Cheese Balls

Corn cheese balls takes 10 minutes of prep time and 20 minutes of cooking time. Serving enough for 2.

Ingridients
Quantity Ingridient Instrusction
1 cup sweet corn boiled and finely chopped
1 cup potato boiled and grated
1/4 cup cheese grated
1 tsp ginger garlic paste optional
1/4 cup refined flour
1 tsp red chilli powder
1 tsp dried oregano, cilantro leaves mixed
salt according to taste
oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In mixing bowl add chopped sweetcorns, grated potato, processed cheese, refined flour, red chilli powder, italian herbs, red chilli powder, salt and mix well.
  2. Make lemon size balls and keep aside. Roll it into refined flour and coat evenly.
  3. Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown in colour and remove onto kitchen absorbent paper.
  4. For the fried rice, heat oil in a pan and add onions and saut√© for a minute.
  5. Serve hot with ketchup.

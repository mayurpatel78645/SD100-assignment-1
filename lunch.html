Chicken Tikka Fried Rice

Chicken tikka fried rice takes fifteen minutes of prep time and 20 minutes of cooking time. Serving enough for 4.

Ingridients
Quantity Ingridient Instrusction
500 gr chicken breast cut into strips
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
1 tsp green chilli chopped
2 tbsp gram flour
1 tbsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp garam masala
2-3 tbsp curd
1 tbsp oil

For rice
1 tbsp oil
1/2 cup onion chopped
1 tbsp ginger garlic chopped
1 tsp green chillies chopped
1/2 cup carrots chopped
1/2 cup bell pepper chopped
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp soy sauce
2 cups boiled rice
as per taste black pepper cracked

Instructions

  1. Take chicken breast pieces in a bowl and add lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, green chillies, gram flour, salt, kashmiri red chilli powder, garam masala and curd. Mix everything well. See to it that the chicken pieces are coated evenly.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated pieces on both the sides till nice tender.
  3. Remove and cut into bite size pieces. Keep aside.
  4. For the fried rice, heat oil in a pan and add onions and saut√© for a minute.
  5. Add ginger, garlic and green chillies to this and mix.
  6. Add chopped carrots and capsicum and toss everything together.
  7. Add sugar and soy sauce and mix everything well.
  8. Add the boiled rice and mix well. Add freshly cracked pepper and the chicken tikka cubes and toss everything together.
  9. serve hot.
